On Sunday, Germany’s far-right opposition party ‘Alternative for Germany’ (AfD) marched in Berlin. Opponents have held multiple rival demonstrations, organised - among others organisations like artists groups, pro-refugee organisations, LGBTQI groups - by a coalition of Berlin music clubs. Participating clubs closed on Sunday and took their visitors to the streets. The resulting parade was supposed to “bass away” the AfD and peacefully disturb their rally.
